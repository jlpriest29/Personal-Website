About

Student

Originally from Long Island, Jesse is a second year Computer Engineering Student at Stevens Institute of Technology in Hoboken, New Jersey. He will graduate with his bachelor's degree in May of 2020. While at school he focuses on his classes while living with a life-long learner mindset, constantly trying to improve. He has a cumulative GPA of 3.75 and is an active member of the Stevens Community.

Developer

Jesse was introduced to programming and development as a freshman in college and has not looked back. His strengths are in front end development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. He also has experience with C++ and is looking forward to continuing to learn more and improve his skills and portfolio. He is constantly working on new projects for the sake of learning as well as having fun.

Leader

With a background in leadership, Jesse is ready to tackle any tasks he may face. An Eagle Scout, Jesse staffed a course called National Youth Leadership Training for two consecutive summers. At college, he is a twice elected Senator of the Student Government Association, the head of the Campus Life Committee, and the Vice President of Finance of his fraternity, Sigma Phi Epsilon New Jersey Alpha Residential Learning Community.
